# SQL Projects

## Project1: SQL-Based Brewery Data Analysis 

### Overview [![SQL](https://img.shields.io/badge/SQL-Analysis-blue?style=flat)](https://github.com/seoyeon3/SQL-Projects/blob/main/brewery_data_analysis.sql)
This project delivers actionable insights from a relational brewery dataset by building a structured SQL-based querying system.  
It enables analysis of market trends, product characteristics, operational patterns, and regional distribution within the beer industry.


## Key Insights Delivered

- **Market Trends**  
  Identified dominant beer styles and historical patterns, helping understand market preferences  

- **Product Insights**  
  Analyzed collaboration beers, high-ABV outliers, and low-rated products to support product strategy and quality assessment  

- **Operational Insights**  
  Detected breweries with limited or no production and those with diverse style portfolios, informing operational efficiency  

- **Regional Insights**  
  Identified cities with the highest concentration of breweries, providing insight into market density and location strategy  

- **User-Focused Querying**  
  Enabled dynamic search (e.g., by style or name), allowing users to directly explore and retrieve relevant business information
